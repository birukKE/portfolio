Project


Metroped

MetroPed is a collaborative project between KTH and Tongji University focused on exploring human perception of crowded public transport spaces as part of sustainable urban development research. Using Unity, me and my team reconstructed a Stockholm subway platform (Rådmansgatan) and populated it with simulated moving crowds. The scene was made fully VR-compatible (SteamVR) while also supporting a non-VR mode.

The main goal was:

  • Reconstruction of a recognizable Stockholm subway environment.
  • Crowd simulation with reactive movement.
  • VR implementation for immersive experiments.
